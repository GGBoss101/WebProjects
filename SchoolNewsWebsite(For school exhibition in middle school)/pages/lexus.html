Lexus Showroom Visit

On the 2nd of April , the Commerce students of Grade 12 and Economics students of Grade 10 were given the opportunity to visit the Lexus showroom on Sheikh Zayed Road and learn more about the marketing and sales promotion strategies . They were explained how Lexus is different from other regular car brands because of its goodwill and high reputation , and the focus of the company on “Experience Amazing” which includes great product quality , quality control , customer service and after sales service . It is rated second on the list of best premium car brands worldwide . The difference between luxury brands or premium brands and commercial brands were also brought out . An interesting fact that was brought up was how customers purchasing luxury brands often do so because of the brand name , and and have a pattern to return to the same brand for future purchases . They could correlate this to the Veblen effect which they have already learnt in Economics, where people tend to purchase more expensive goods because of the distinction and status it gives them . The saleswoman at Lexus also explained to them the different departments at Lexus , from Logistics , to Sales and Marketing . She also elaborated on how it is important to cater to the generations most common in the market and pointed out two examples . One with reference to their hybrid cars , where she explained how they haven’t fully converted to electric cars because the market currently is more comfortable with fuel cars and as they transition and want more electric cars , the company shall move in that direction . Another example was how despite the growing popularity of social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook , traditional advertising on billboards and poster is still necessary to cater to the part of society who aren’t active on such websites , but still form the target market of Lexus . They also visited Mall of the Emirates and visited Carrefour , the leading supermarket chain in UAE , to understand more of the sales strategies implemented and overall product display and how it integrates into the marketing plan of the company . It was really enriching for them to be given a chance to interrelate concepts we have already learnt it class and understand how it is actually applied in the industry . The students enjoyed honing their knowledge about the working of commercial businesses in real life.
