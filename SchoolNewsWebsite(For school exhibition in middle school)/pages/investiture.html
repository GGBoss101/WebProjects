Investiture

Investiture Ceremony- “LEADERSHIP AND LEARNING ARE INDISPENSABLE TO EACH OTHER”- The investiture ceremony is a new improvised process of electing members of the Student Council. An Investiture ceremony that honoured 50 student council members of the Senior School and Middle School.The investiture ceremony signifies reliance and confidence that the school consigns in the newly invested office bearers. Donning the mantle of accountability, they also pledge to bestow their duties to the best of their abilities.
The aim is:
To empower students to work in partnership with staff towards shared goals. To enable creativity to flourish as the school community benefits from the wealth of experiences, ideas, skills and sense of fun that students bring. To provide opportunities for students to develop leadership skills through a variety of inspiring, challenging and valued projects which impact positively on learning, teaching and well-being for both students and staff at JSSIS. To prepare students for Higher Education and for achieving personal and professional excellence in life after formal education. The morning of 18th April 2019 brought with it a lot of celebration and excitement for the JSSIS family.The ceremony in JSSIS was held with great zeal and zest. The newly chosen head boy Adityan Rajan proudly carried the school flag followed by House Council Members carrying their respective house flags on the stage. All the members of student council were conferred with badges and sashes by honourable Principal mam Mrs.Lata Nakra. Mrs. Lata Nakra congratulated and advised the members of council to be impartial and honest in discharging their duties and to be the role models for the rest of students. The function concluded with the National Anthem.
