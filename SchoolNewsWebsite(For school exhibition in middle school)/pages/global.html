Bharat Parvah

To keep the culture and spirit of our nation high in our hearts, the students of JSSIS performed in “Bharat Parva” organized by the Indian Consulate on the occasion of 70th Indian Republic Day. They rocked the stage. Their teacher Azeem sir did a great job in choreographing the steps from the famous floss to the trendy hype . The students worked very hard and put up a great show. We appreciate their effort and recognize the hard work behind it. The Indian Consulate Event was one of immense celebration. An occasion that brought about pride in not only the nation but in our tradition culture and values .The stage was lit up with dances acts and songs by various schools and institutions alike.After finishing our performance we felt rich with patriotism.The Republic Day at the Indian Consulate is one to truly experience
